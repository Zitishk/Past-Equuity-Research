# Indian 2D Animation — Key Figures Directory
*For Advisory, Partnership & Industry Entry Strategy*
*Research Date: March 2026 | Compiled for Luminary Frame Studios (Proposed)*

---

> **Purpose:** To identify Indian persons with decades of experience and deep industry ties in 2D animation — senior animators, directors, and producers — who could serve as advisors, co-founders, or industry entry points for a new indie animation studio.

---

## TIER 1: FOUNDING-GENERATION PIONEERS (Historical Reference / Deceased)

These figures define the lineage. Understanding their legacy — and approaching their successors — is foundational to credibility in the industry.

---

**"The Father of Indian Animation"**

| | |
|---|---|
| **Roles** | Founder, Ram Mohan Biographics (1972); Co-founder, Graphiti Multimedia (1995) |
| **Key 2D Works** | *Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama* (1992); *Meena* (UNICEF); 3× National Award films; Amul Girl mascot; Air India Maharaja mascot; title sequences for Satyajit Ray, Gulzar, Mrinal Sen |

### Two Key Events That Access Most of This List Simultaneously

**1. AniMela** (January–February, Mumbai)
- Organized by Kireet Khurana's foundation in partnership with the Annecy Festival
- Draws Kireet, Vaibhav (TASI), and the broader indie community
- Sponsoring or exhibiting creates warm introductions without cold outreach

**2. FICCI FRAMES** (March, Mumbai)
- Munjal Shroff chairs the AVGC-XR Forum sessions
- Attracts studio heads, OTT buyers, government policy makers
- FICCI membership (~₹10,000–25,000/year for startups) gets you into the room

---

### Low-Cost Entry: ASIFA India Membership
- 15 sub-chapters across India (Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Delhi, Chennai, Indore, etc.)
- Nominal membership fee; instant access to working animators, educators, and events
- **International Animation Day** (October 28, held in 15+ cities) — industry mixer in every major city
- WAVES Festival (May, Mumbai) — now a government-backed mega-event with animation track
